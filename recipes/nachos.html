Nachos

Description

Prepare for the best tasting nachos you've ever tasted in your life! From the crunch of the chips, to the yummy goodness of the melted cheese. From the sweet, savory taste of the corn, to the firey zing of the jalapenos. Your taste buds will thank you for this mouth watering rollercoaster ride.

Ingredients

  • Chips
  • Tomatoes
  • One can "14 oz." Black Beans
  • 1 lb of Impossible Beef
  • One can "14 oz." sweet corn
  • Half a red onion, chopped
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese to taste
  • 1/2 cup of Cilantro, chopped.
  • Olives to taste
  • Jalapenos to taste

Steps

  • Cook the Impossible Meat
  • Open both cans, heat up black beans
  • Chop the half onion, cilantro, tomatoes, olives and Jalapenos
  • After everything is chopped and the meat and beans are cooked, simply make yourself a plate of chips and start pooring everything on! "My advice is to start with the meat and beans, then put the cheese directly over the hot food in order to melt the cheese". And that's it! you are done! nice and easy recipe to feed a whole family in less than 30 minutes!
