Nachos

Description

Prepare for the best tasting nachos you've ever tasted in your life! From the crunch of the chips, to the yummy goodness of the melted cheese. From the sweet, savory taste of the corn, to the firey zing of the jalapenos. Your taste buds will thank you for this mouth watering rollercoaster ride.

Ingredients

  • Chips
  • Tomatoes
  • One can "14 oz." Black Beans
  • 1 lb of Impossible Beef
  • One can "14 oz." sweet corn
  • Half a red onion, chopped
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese to taste
  • 1/2 cup of Cilantro, chopped.
  • Olives to taste
  • Jalapenos to taste

Steps

  • Cook the Impossible Meat
  • Open both cans, heat up black beans
  • Chop the half onion, cilantro, tomatoes, olives and Jalapenos
  • After everything is chopped and the meat and beans are cooked, simply make yourself a plate of chips and start pooring everything on! "My advice is to start with the meat and beans, then put the cheese directly over the hot food in order to melt the cheese". And that's it! you are done! nice and easy recipe to feed a whole family in less than 30 minutes!

Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

These arent your typical peanut butter cookies with loads of unnecessary ingrediaents. These cookies have 3 ingredients only!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
  • Combine the peanut butter, sugar and egg. Mix until smooth.
  • Drop spoonfulls of cookie dough onto the prepared sheet. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 6-8 minutes. Don not overbake! These cookies are best when they are still soft and just barely brown on the bottoms.

Dessert Crepes

Description

This crepe recipe is essential for a fancy breakfast or eye-catching dessert. Sprinkle warm crepes with sugar and lemon, or serve with whipped cream, ice cream, and fruit.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  • Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until smooth.
  • Heat a medium skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel.
  • Using a serving spoon or small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons crepe batter onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface. Cook until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side; serve warm.
